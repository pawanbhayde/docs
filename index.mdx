## What is Saanchar?

**Saanchar** is a Marketing & Communication SaaS platform that helps businesses send and manage **bulk messaging campaigns** across channels like **WhatsApp and Email** from a single dashboard or via developer APIs.

Instead of integrating multiple messaging services individually, businesses connect their communication channels to Saanchar and manage everything in one centralized system.

<Tip>
Saanchar acts as a unified communication infrastructure for marketing, notifications, and customer engagement.
</Tip>

---

## Core Idea

Saanchar simplifies business communication.

Companies often need multiple messaging systems such as WhatsApp APIs, email infrastructure, campaign tools, and analytics dashboards. Managing all of these separately increases complexity and cost.

With Saanchar, businesses:

- Connect their messaging channels
- Manage campaigns
- Send messages in bulk
- Monitor delivery and engagement

—all from one platform.

---

## Main Capabilities

### 1. WhatsApp Marketing

Businesses can connect their **WhatsApp Business API (WABA)** accounts and run messaging campaigns.

Features include:

- Connect WhatsApp Business API accounts
- Send template-based messages
- Broadcast bulk campaigns
- Manage customer conversations
- Track delivery status and engagement

---

### 2. Email Marketing

Saanchar also enables businesses to run **email marketing campaigns** using verified sending domains.

Capabilities include:

- Add and verify sending domains (e.g., AWS SES)
- Send bulk email campaigns
- Track email opens, clicks, and delivery
- Create and manage email templates

---

### 3. Campaign Management

Users can create and manage marketing campaigns directly from the dashboard.

Typical workflow:

1. Create a campaign
2. Upload or import contact lists
3. Schedule the campaign or send instantly
4. Monitor analytics such as sent, delivered, and failed messages

---

## Developer APIs

Saanchar provides APIs that allow developers to integrate messaging directly into their applications.

Common use cases include:

- Sending OTP verification codes
- Transactional notifications
- Marketing automation
- Order status updates
- Customer alerts

```bash
POST /api/v1/whatsapp/send
````

<Tip>
APIs allow SaaS platforms and applications to automate messaging without using the dashboard.
</Tip>

## Platform Components

### Dashboard

The dashboard is the web interface where users manage their communication workflows.

Users can:

* Connect messaging channels
* Create campaigns
* Manage contacts
* View campaign analytics and delivery reports

---

### Messaging Engine

The messaging engine is the backend service responsible for sending messages at scale.

Responsibilities include:

* Processing campaigns
* Queueing messages
* Delivering messages via WhatsApp or Email APIs
* Handling retries and failures

---

### Contact Management

Saanchar includes a contact management system for storing and organizing customer data.

This includes:

* Customer contact lists
* Segments and groups
* Campaign recipients

---

## Target Users

Saanchar is designed for organizations that require scalable messaging infrastructure.

Typical users include:

* Startups
* SaaS platforms
* E-commerce companies
* Marketing teams
* Customer support teams

---

## Example Workflow

A typical campaign workflow looks like this:

1. A business signs up on Saanchar.
2. The business connects a WhatsApp Business API account or verifies an email sending domain.
3. Contacts are uploaded or imported into the platform.
4. A campaign is created and scheduled or sent instantly.
5. Saanchar processes the campaign and sends messages in bulk.
6. The user monitors delivery analytics and campaign performance from the dashboard.
